Ember is for anyone who keeps notes. A trader keeps their lessons. A student keeps their courses. A founder keeps their ideas.

See it your way

A canvas for your thinking.

Notes, blocks, and a mind map on one open canvas. Ember lays your ideas out the way your head actually works. No plugins to install, no setup to learn. It just works.

Real terminal power

A full terminal, right in your notebook.

Run real commands and AI agents next to your notes. No second app, no window to switch to. Drag a note or a file straight into the terminal and keep going. Terminal power, wrapped in a beautiful app.

Teach it once

Show Ember a skill once. It keeps it forever.

Teach Ember how you like something done, a way to lay out a note or a routine you run every week, and it remembers. You never explain it twice.

Plan toward a day

Tell it the deadline. It plans the weeks.

Point Ember at a day, like an exam or a trip, and it spreads the work across every week between now and then. A little at a time, so nothing lands the night before.
